About Temple

Kamakhya Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kamakhya, located on Nilachal Hill in Guwahati,ASSAM. It is one of the 51 sacred Shakti Peethas and is an important center of Shakti worship and Tantra. The temple is known for its unique sanctum, where a natural rock fissure is worshipped as the goddess. Every year, thousands of devotees visit the temple, especially during the Ambubachi Mela festival. The temple is one of the most important religious and cultural landmarks in Assam.

Temple History

Kamakhya Mandir is one of the most sacred Shakti Peethas in India, located on Nilachal Hill in Guwahati, Assam.

Darshan Timings

The temple is open daily for devotees. Morning and evening darshan timings may vary during festivals.

Ambubachi Mela

Ambubachi Mela is the most famous festival celebrated at Kamakhya Temple and attracts pilgrims from across India.
