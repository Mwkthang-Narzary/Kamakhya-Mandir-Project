About Us

Welcome to the Kamakhya Mandir website. This website is created as a student web development project to provide information about the famous Kamakhya Temple located in Guwahati, Assam.

Our aim is to share details about the temple’s history, festivals, darshan timings, and cultural importance in a simple and user-friendly way.

This website helps visitors and devotees explore important information about the temple in one place.
