🌺 Durga Puja

Festival of Strength, Power & Victory of Good over Evil

🪔 About Durga Puja

Durga Puja is one of the most important Hindu festivals, celebrated mainly in Assam, West Bengal, Odisha, and other eastern states. It is dedicated to Goddess Durga, the symbol of strength, courage, and divine feminine energy. The festival marks the victory of Goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura, representing the triumph of good over evil.

📖 Celebration of Durga Puja

Durga Puja is celebrated for ten days, usually in September or October. The last five days are the most important — Shashthi, Saptami, Ashtami, Navami, and Vijayadashami. People visit beautifully decorated pandals, offer prayers, enjoy cultural programs, and participate in rituals with great devotion.
